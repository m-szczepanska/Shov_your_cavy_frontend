About

The story of Squeakoo

I started out wanting to write a simple hobby project with Django REST as an exercise (and to pad my portfolio). When working on the first models I envisioned expanding the project and creating a Tinder-like app for animals in animal shelters. The Tinder-like app turned out more complex than expected and has been shelved... for now.

I created Squeakoo as a stepping stone towards that app - a simple site for cavy (for civilians: guinea pigs) owners where they can share the photos of their cavies and rate other user piggies.

The app is obviously not perfect - but it is launched, and that counts for something.

May the Squeak be with you,
